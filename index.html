What's New?

Last month I attended my first hackathon, HackMIT, and worked with a team from Williams team to create a lecture-transcribing site and study tool. Although everyone's coding was pretty rushed, I did enjoy how the hackathon format forces you to work through each stage of the development process in a short period of time. It certainly helped me realize the strengths and flaws of our idea in only one night, which is an excellent place to begin a further, more rigorous expansion of the app. Future hackathons should also be less intimidating now that I know the ropes, and I'm excited to be gearing up for BostonHacks next month!
